T-MATS Block Guide

The toolbox for the modeling and analysis of thermodynamic systems (T-MATS)is a Simulink toolbox intended for use in the modeling and simulation of thermodyanmic systems and their controls. This block guide has been created to offer details on the blocks within the T-MATS package.

Below is the table of contents containing links to the various sections. These links may also be accessed for any T-MATS block by double clicking the block and selecting help.
